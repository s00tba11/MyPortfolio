Data Visualisation - Northwind Analysis

Project information

Category: Data Analytics

Client: NYP

Project date: 01 November, 2022

Project Scope:

The objective of this project is to analyse the Northwind database and provide insights to the company. The insights will help the company to make better business decisions.

As a team we are required to design and build scalable data models, clean and transform data and design easy-to-comprehend data visualisations with the use of Power Bi. The five areas of analysis are:

  • Customer Analysis
  • Employee Analysis
  • Product Analysis
  • Suppliers Analysis
  • Shipper Analysis

My Role & Solution: As the team leader, I'm tasked with managing the team, allocating the tasks, ensuring that we are on track and can complete the assigment on time. Additionally, I'm in charge of product analysis, integration, and the overall design of the analysis dashboards shown in the pictures.

Creating Visuals:

  • Create a visualisation showing the overview of sales by product category

  • Create a visualisation showing the product(s) that have the highest number of quantities sold and generate the highest sales
